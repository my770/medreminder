Help

Welcome to the Medication Reminder App! Follow the instructions below to get started:

Adding Medications

  • Click the Add Paracetamol and/or Add Ibuprofen button on the Medications tab to enter the details for a specific medication.
  • Select the date and time of the last dose using the provided input field.

Submitting and Viewing Schedule

  • Once you've added the details for one or both medications, click Submit to save your entries.
  • Navigate to the Schedule tab to view a detailed timeline of the next doses for each medication.

Understanding the Timeline

  • The timeline shows a clear breakdown of when each medication should be given, ensuring you don't miss a dose.
  • Past doses are marked with a strikethrough for easy differentiation.

Resetting or Editing

  • If you need to change the schedule, go back to the Medications tab and enter the updated details.
  • Submit the form again to refresh the timeline.
